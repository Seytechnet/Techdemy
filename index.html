Ethical Hacking Training FAQs

  • Training Prerequisites
  • Time & Mode of Delivery
Q. What software/tools would be needed for the training and how can I get them?
All the software/tools that you need for the training would be shared with you during the training as and when you need it.
Q. Do I need to have a laptop or desktop to do the training or can I do it on mobile too?
You can watch the videos and take quizzes & tests on any device including mobile. However, for assignments and practice, use of desktop or laptop is required.
Q. What system configuration is needed to do this training?
To do this training, you would need a computer with a minimum 1 GB ram and Windows 8 or later (64 bit).
Q. What are the prerequisites for the training?
There are no prerequisites for this training as it is of beginner level. Everything that you need to know about Ethical Hacking would be taught to you from scratch.
Q. When can I start the training?
You can choose your preferred batch date while signing up for the training program and start accordingly.
Q. What will be the timings of the training?
As this is a purely online training program, you can choose to learn at any time of the day and for as much time as you want. We will recommend a pace to be followed throughout the program, but the actual timings and learning hours are in your hands.
Q. What will be the language of delivery for this train
The text chapters, quizzes, assignments, and projects will be in English only.
Q. What is the training duration or what does 8 weeks access to the platform mean?
From your batch start date, you will have access to training's content (videos, quizzes, tests, assignments, projects etc.) in the platform for 8 weeks with-in which you should be able to complete your training.
Q. How much time should I spend on the training every day to be able to complete it in 8 weeks?
As this is a purely online training program, you can choose to spend as much time as you want per day and the more time you spend, sooner you would be able to complete the training. However, on an average, spending 1-2 hours per day should be sufficient for you to complete the training in 8 weeks. Some students also choose to devote more time on the weekends in case they are not able to give time on the weekdays.
